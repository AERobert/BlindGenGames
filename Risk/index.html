      Card Trading

      Valid sets: 3 of same type, one of each type (Infantry+Cavalry+Artillery), or 2 of any type + Wild.
      Press T during reinforcement to trade manually (3-4 cards).
      At 5+ cards, you must trade — the game auto-trades for you.
      Trading a card for a territory you own places +2 armies directly on that territory.
